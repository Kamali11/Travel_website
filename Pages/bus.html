Frequently Asked Questions
Can I track the location of my booked bus online?

Yes, you can track your bus online by using our bus tracking app feature called Track My Bus. This feature allows passengers and their families to track the live bus tracking location. You may follow your bus on a map and use the information to plan your trip to the boarding point and to get off at the correct stop. Family and friends may also check the bus position to plan pick-ups and ensure your safety.

Do I need to create an account on the Happytrip site to book my bus ticket?

No, you don,t have to create an account on Happytrip site to book your bus ticket. But it is advisable to create one to accelerate the process next time you want to book bus tickets. Also Happytrip has many discounts and offers that you can easily access if you have an account with us.

What's New in Bus Booking on HappyTrip?

Primo Bus Ticket: Happytrip has launched Primo bus services, where passengers can enjoy traveling in high-rated buses with best-in-class services. While looking for bus tickets on the desired route, customers can check the Primo tag to choose this excellent service. From hygiene standards to on-time service and comfort, passengers can benefit from online bus booking experience from Primo buses.

Can I change the date of my journey after I have booked my bus ticket?

Yes. You can change the date of journey after booking bus ticket on Happytrip by clicking the “Reschedule” icon if you think your travel plan might get interrupted while booking. Bus operators with the “Reschedule icon” next to it offer rescheduling of the bus ticket in case your initially selected date is not viable to travel on.

I've lost my ticket. What should I do now?

A copy of the bus ticket would have been sent to you by email when you booked bus ticket online . Please take a printout of that mail and produce it at the time of boarding. If you have not received the ticket e-mail, please call any of our call centers and our executive will resend you a copy by mail.

Is it safe to use my credit or debit card to buy bus tickets on HappyTrip?

Transactions on Happytrip are very safe. We employ the best-in-class security and the transactions done are secure. Apart from being certified by Verisign, Happytrip uses Secure Socket Layers (SSL) data encryption. \ Using SSL ensures that the information exchanged with us is never transmitted unencrypted, thus protecting the information from being viewed by unauthorized individuals.

What are the different payment options available on Bus Ticket booking?

There are many payment modes available to book buses on the Happytrip website and app. Some of these payment modes that are available for the Happytrip booking process are:

  • Debit Card/Credit Card/ATM Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Maestro & Rupay)
  • Net Banking (HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Indian Bank, Axis Bank, SBI and all major banks)
  • UPI (Google Pay, Amazon Pay, PhonePe)
  • Book Now, Pay Later (Simple)
  • Wallets (Paytm)

Make sure to check the coupon code to get the discounts on bus booking online, Happytrip offers a lot of HappyDeals to book the bus ticket on budget.

How can I get a refund in case I cancel my ticket?

The refund is provided as per cancellation policy of the operator. The refund can be credited to the source of payment (Example: debit card, credit card, net banking) or credited to redBus wallet. Wallet credit can be used for bus booking in future (within 6 months of cancellation).

How can I reschedule my bus tickets?

To reschedule your bus ticket on Happytrip, follow these steps:

  • 1.Visit https://www.Happytrip.in/reschedule
  • 2.Search for your bus ticket by entering your ticket number and registered email ID.
  • 3.Verify your online bus tickets details and select the date to reschedule your journey.
  • 4.Select a bus operator, verify every detail, and make your payment if any difference in bus ticket prices needs to be cleared.
